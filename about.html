Welcome to PawTrack!🐕🐾

What was missing before PawTrack

Dog owners in Kenya and across East Africa have long faced a fragmented, unreliable experience when seeking professional care for their dogs. We listened. Here is what we kept hearing.
❌No reliable way to verify a trainer's credentials or track record before handing over your dog
❌Zero visibility into what happens during training — owners paid and hoped for the best
❌No progress records — training milestones lived in a trainer's notebook, if at
❌Owners traveling abroad had no way to check on their boarded dogs remotely
❌Mating services were scattered, unverified, and often unsafe for the animals involved
